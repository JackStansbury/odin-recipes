Sesame Balls

Description

When I go to dim sum, I look forward to warm, crispy sesame balls with red bean filling more than any other dish. Which is saying something because I fucking love a lot of other stuff at dim sum.

It's just the perfect combination of textures and flavors. The outer crust is hot, crispy, and savory while still being light. Biting into gives way to a soft, steamy interior surrounding the treasure at the middle - sweet red bean paste! So fucking good, I'm actually getting hungry just writing this haha.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 1.5 cups chili peppers
  • 1.5 tbsp. Sichuan peppercorn
  • 1 root ginger, sliced

Steps

  1. Cube chicken and fry in hot oil until golden brown
  2. Remove chicken and transfer to paper towel lined plate
  3. Fry the chili peppers & sichuan peppercorn in oil for 1-2 minutes
  4. Add ginger, scallion, and garlic and fry until aromatic
  5. Add in cooked chicken and stir to combine
